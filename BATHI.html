About the BATHI Lab

The BATHI Lab focuses on the intersection of Behavior Analysis and technology to tackle health-related problems such as recovery from total knee replacement surgery and neuromuscular training. By using innovative technology and behavior analytic principles, we aim to re-imagine the field of rehabilitation and medicine for the better.
